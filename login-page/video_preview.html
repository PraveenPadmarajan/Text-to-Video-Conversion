Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I generate a video?

To generate a video, follow these steps:

  1. Enter your text in the provided text box.
  2. Choose the desired settings such as language, voice, and video format.
  3. Click the "Generate Video" button.
  4. Once the video is generated, you can download it in different formats.

How can I change the voice of the narration?

You can change the voice of the narration by selecting a different voice option in the settings menu.
